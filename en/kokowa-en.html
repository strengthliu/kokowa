About KOKOWA®.

What's KOKOWA®?

KOKOWA® (WT1 tumor Vaccine, ) helps protect you against tumor with WT1-expressing. High expression of the WT1 gene in leukemias and solid tumors indicated that the WT1 protein might be a promising tumor-associated antigen (TAA).

KOKOWA® may not fully protect everyone, nor will it protect against tumor not caused by WT1 gene.

KOKOWA®does not prevent all types of cancer, so it’s important for you to continue routine cancer screenings. KOKOWA® does not treat cancer.

KOKOWA®is a shot that is usually given in the armpit under the skin. KOKOWA®may be given as 2 or 3 shots.

For persons 30 through 60 years of age, KOKOWA®is given using a 3-dose schedule; the second shot should be given 14days after the first shot and the third shot should be given 1 months after the first shot.

The appropriate dosing schedule will be determined by you health care professional.
