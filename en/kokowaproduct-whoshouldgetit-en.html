About KOKOWA®

Who is suitable for the injection of WT1 vaccine?

1. Patients who prevent recurrence after surgery;

2. Tumor-susceptible population: including family history of cancer, positive tumor susceptibility gene detection, etc.;

3. Bad living habits (smoking, alcohol abuse, bad eating habits), excessive stress during long-term work;

Only a doctor or health care professional can decide if KOKOWA® is right for you.
