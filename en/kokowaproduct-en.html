About KOKOWA®

KOKOWA® Product Description

●【Name】

Broad-spectrum tumor vaccine

●【Components】

Broad-spectrum, highly efficient pluripotent WT1 antigen, optimized in vivo microenvironment adjuvant, cytokines.

●【Specifications】

Injection: 100μl milky white liquid.

●【Expected Usage】

The broad-spectrum tumor vaccine is mainly used for the prevention of tumors. It has a preventive effect on multiple myeloma of leukemia, non-hodgkin's lymphoma, lung cancer, gastric cancer, pancreatic cancer, cholangiocarcinoma, gallbladder cancer, colorectal cancer, kidney cancer, prostate cancer, thyroid cancer, melanoma, uterine body cancer, cervical cancer, glioma and other tumors.

●【Function Principle】

Tumor vaccine is a kind of induced by enhancing or specific response to the tumor cells of the immune treatment using broad-spectrum WT antigen preparation of anti-tumor vaccine, promote the activation of T lymphocyte proliferation and cytokine release, in order to achieve the purpose of preventing cancer when tumor vaccines injection to nearby lymph nodes, interleukin 2 (IL - 2) on T cells to the injection point and activation of T cells; Granulocyte chemotactic factor gm-csf dendritic cells (DC) chemotaxis to injection point, consuming processing WT1 antigen, and give T WT1 antigen presenting cells, in view of the killer T cell antigen of WT1 and memory T cells, once again met with WT1 antigen in the cancer cells and killer T cells can exert its cytotoxic effect, kill cancer cells, so as to achieve the purpose of preventing cancer.

●【Storage method and validity period】

Store in the dark at 2-8 °C. Validity: One year.

●【Usage and Treatment Course】

1. Intradermal or subcutaneous injection near the axillary or inguinal lymph nodes.

2. One injection per injection, the injection dose is 100μl.

3. The injection point spacing is 2cm.

If injected near the same lymph node group, each injection point must be at least 2 cm apart.

One preventive course is 2-4 needles.

The broad-spectrum tumor vaccine can be used alone or in combination with any clinical treatment.

●【Indications】

1. Patients who prevent recurrence after surgery;

2. Tumor-susceptible population: including family history of cancer, positive tumor susceptibility gene detection, etc.;

3. Bad living habits (smoking, alcohol abuse, bad eating habits), excessive stress during long-term work;

●【Adverse Reactions】

A small number of patients with local injection will appear mild swelling heat pain.

●【Cautions】

It is contraindicated in patients with autoimmune diseases.
